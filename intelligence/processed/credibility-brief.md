## Credibility Assessment

### Strengths
1. SEC-registered since 2012 with verified registration number and TIN
2. MPSA 251(A)-2007-XI is a real, government-issued mining tenement (approved 2007)
3. MGB Central Office approved GMC as authorized operator (May 2022)
4. All operating contracts registered with MGB Region XI with documentary stamps
5. Active PHILEXPORT membership (current through Dec 2026)
6. Detailed DMPF with engineering-grade feasibility analysis
7. Existing assets of P316M as of Dec 2020
8. Active equipment procurement (USD 180K magnetic separators, Jan 2026)
9. Capital increase from P10M to P200M authorized capital (2022)

### Concerns
1. Family-controlled entity (Tupas/Mendiola families)
2. Prior conflict with MPSA holder DMC (criminal case, cease-and-desist) — resolved Feb 2022
3. LOI and copper contract are non-binding/unsigned templates
4. MOU with Aboitiz is blank template for wind power, not mining
5. No evidence of actual mining production, revenue, or shipments
6. DMPF projections (78% IRR, P13.4B NPV) are extremely optimistic and unverified
7. MPSA is "under exploration period" — not yet in production phase
8. Only partial DMPF provided (Sections 4-6, missing geological/resource sections)

### Overall Assessment
GMC is a **legitimate, SEC-registered mining company** with a valid MPSA covering nearly 6,000 hectares in Davao Oriental. The regulatory paperwork is in order. However, it is a **pre-production company** — no mining revenue has been generated. The deal opportunity is real but early-stage. The DMPF financials should be treated as projections requiring independent verification, not as established performance.
