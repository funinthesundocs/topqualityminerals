## Business Description and Scope

GMC is engaged in the **mining, quarrying, processing, and trading of iron ore** as its principal business mission. Iron ore is described as one of the Philippines' high-value mineral products for export.

### Production Capacity
- **Maximum production capacity:** 300,000 metric tons of iron ore per month
- **Trading capacity:** 100,000 metric tons per month

### Quality Assurance
- Extracted ores undergo rigid inspection by partner laboratories:
  - **CCIC (Phils.), Inc.** -- Philippines-based

## Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

GMC's CSR activities center on its relationship with the **Lumad** (indigenous) community of Mati, Davao Oriental:

- **Employment:** Lumads constitute approximately **60% of GMC's total field workforce**
- **Roles provided:** Mining laborers, road mappers and guides, stockyard personnel, property custodians
- **Community support:** Contributions to food security, livelihood, housing, and other basic needs
- **Reforestation:** GMC operates a forest tree nursery tended by the Lumad community to reforest land affected by mining activities
- **Environmental conservation:** Road signs to protect wildlife, careful transplanting and propagation of wild pine, hardwood, and fruit-bearing seedlings to alternative green areas
- **Safety:** Rigorous observance of national and international safety standards; staff training in league with government institutions

### Planned Foundation
GMC is establishing a Foundation for the Lumad and other catch-basin communities to provide:
- Literacy education
- Livelihood training
- Enterprise creation and marketing
- Socialized housing
- Primary health care

*Prepared by: Samuel V. Sendon, Registered Geologist, PRC Reg. No. 000607*

**Q1 2023 (Jan-Mar)** — Pre-Drilling Phase:

**Q2 2023 (Apr-Jun)** — First Drilling Campaign:
- 4 diamond drill holes completed by **Mega Phils.** using **2 simultaneous drill rigs**

**Geological Framework** (from both reports):
- MPSA area underlain by meta-volcanic/sedimentary rocks intruded by hornblende diorite stock
- Iron skarn mineralization concentrated at mid-NW portion; gold mineralization genetically related to iron skarn
